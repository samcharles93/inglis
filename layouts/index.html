Based in the north-west coast from the shores of Burnie, to Wynyard’s stunning Table Cape, Inglis Support Service is dedicated to providing only the most comprehensive, caring support for our clients, celebrating the our key values in respecting choice and independence; we believe in providing only the fullest service for all our clients…

Our Services


Respite

Our respite services are here to help you, to take care of your loved ones when you need a break, need time for activities or just to relax, deal with stresses of everyday life and look after yourself.


Tenancy Support

With our tenancy support services we can help you find the right location and accommodation, we can represent you in meetings with real estate agencies, help you complete your application and help arrange inspections, moving, purchasing furniture and getting utilities connected.


Community Access

At Inglis Support, we believe that getting involved with community activities, events and outings help our clients foster a strong community presence, help build practical skills, develop strong interpersonal relationships and most importantly, have fun.
